About

From human expertise to AI-powered intelligence—reshaping how the world learns, preserves, and creates knowledge.

Vision

Reimagining the Future of Knowledge and Intelligence

We envision a future where expert knowledge becomes scalable, accessible, and continuously evolving beyond individuals and institutions.

AI+Expert bridges human expertise and artificial intelligence to transform how knowledge is created, learned, and applied.

In this future, AI systems embody expert thinking, enabling global access to high-level knowledge and expanding human potential.

Mission

Building the Infrastructure for Scalable Expert Intelligence

Our mission is to systematically capture, structure, and model expert knowledge into AI-driven systems.

We develop Expert Digital Twins and intelligent learning environments to scale mentorship and practice-based education.

Through global collaboration, we build an ecosystem where expertise can be preserved, shared, and continuously expanded.

Framework

What is AI+Expert

AI+Expert is a research-driven initiative that transforms expert knowledge into structured, AI-enabled systems.

It develops Expert Digital Twins that capture and simulate expert thinking, decision-making, and practice.

By integrating AI, education, and cultural heritage, the project enables scalable learning, knowledge preservation, and global collaboration.

Context

Why It Matters

Expert knowledge is often tacit, difficult to capture, and limited in scale.

Traditional transmission models cannot meet the demands of a global, digital, and rapidly evolving world.

AI+Expert addresses this gap by transforming expertise into scalable, AI-driven systems for learning, preservation, and innovation.
