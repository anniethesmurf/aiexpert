Heritage Project Atlas

The Heritage Project Atlas gathers AI-enabled heritage initiatives into a connected project view, showing how cultural practice, field contexts, and digital systems intersect.

It helps institutions, researchers, and learners trace how projects evolve from local expertise into structured knowledge systems.
