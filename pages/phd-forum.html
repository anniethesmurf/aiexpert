PhD Forum

The PhD Forum is a dedicated academic platform for doctoral researchers working at the intersection of AI, education, and expert knowledge systems.

It supports the development of emerging research in AI+Expert, enabling PhD students to explore how human expertise can be captured, modeled, and transformed into scalable AI-driven learning systems.

We are shaping the next generation of research in human-AI collaborative intelligence.

Research Topics

Adaptive learning systems · Intelligent tutoring systems · Learning analytics

AI+Expert Systems

Expert knowledge modeling · Digital Twin systems · Human-AI collaboration

Cultural Heritage & Knowledge Systems

Intangible cultural heritage modeling · Tacit knowledge representation · Digital humanities

Learning Sciences & Interaction

Cognitive modeling · Learning experience design · Human-centered AI

Academic Network

The forum builds an academic network of doctoral researchers, supervisors, labs, and partner institutions working across AI, education, and expert knowledge systems.

Mentorship and exchange connect doctoral research to a wider international research community.

Publications

Publication pathways include conference papers, journal articles, joint reports, and prototype-linked research outputs emerging from doctoral work.
