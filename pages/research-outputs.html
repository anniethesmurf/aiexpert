Research Outputs (Heritage Specific)

Heritage-specific outputs translate project work into publications, datasets, case studies, learning modules, and prototype systems.

These outputs connect cultural practice with AI in Education, knowledge modelling, and human-AI collaboration research.

Output Types

Case studies

Detailed accounts of heritage projects, modelling approaches, and expert collaboration processes.

Research papers

Academic outputs spanning AIED, knowledge systems, digital humanities, and design research.

Datasets and documentation

Structured evidence for future modelling, analysis, and educational use.

Prototype systems

Demonstrators such as digital twins, learning experiences, and AI-supported interpretation tools.

Research Value

Heritage outputs help show how AI+Expert methods work in rich, culturally grounded contexts where tacit knowledge, material practice, and interpretation matter deeply.

Research outputs make heritage collaboration legible across scholarship, systems design, and future implementation.
