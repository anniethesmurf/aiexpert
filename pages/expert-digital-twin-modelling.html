Expert Digital Twin Modelling

Expert Digital Twin Modelling focuses on representing how heritage masters observe, decide, sequence, and perform specialized practice.

The goal is not only to document what experts know, but to model how they think and act in real contexts.

Modelling Dimensions

Gesture and process

Capturing movement, sequencing, and embodied technique.

Decision structures

Mapping how experts interpret materials, context, and variation.

Tacit knowledge

Representing what is often difficult to verbalize but central to practice.

Interactive outputs

Translating models into tutor agents, simulations, and learning tools.

Digital Twin Training Process

How heritage knowledge becomes structured and scalable AI systems

This process illustrates how real-world heritage practice is captured, modeled, and transformed into AI-driven learning systems.

Example: Batik Digital Twin Training Process

Twin workflow

  1. Observe expert practice
  2. Capture interaction and process
  3. Model expert reasoning
  4. Construct digital twin
  5. Deploy in learning systems
  6. Validate with experts

Digital twins make heritage expertise portable, teachable, and scalable without disconnecting it from lived practice.
